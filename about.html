Our Story

the origin of thai aroma

Our family’s love for Thai cooking began generations ago in a small village near Chiang Mai, where Grandma Malee cooked every meal with fresh herbs from her garden and recipes passed down through time. When we moved to the U.S., we brought those flavors with us—along with her passion for sharing meals that bring people together. In 2010, we opened our doors to the community, offering authentic Thai cuisine made with heart, heritage, and the same family recipes. Today, every dish we serve is a tribute to her legacy and the joy of gathering around a warm, flavorful meal.
